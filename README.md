# Sports Our Youth — Official Website & Fundraising Platform

Sports Our Youth is a mission-driven organization focused on ensuring every young athlete has the opportunity to grow, compete, and lead—regardless of their family’s financial situation. This repository contains the source code for the official Sports Our Youth website, including core informational pages, donation flows (UI), and a merch-based giving experience via an integrated store (UI).

Our platform is built to be clear, credible, and easy to use—so families, partners, and donors can confidently support youth programs and expand access to high-quality coaching, equipment, nutrition, and travel opportunities.

---

## Mission & Vision

### Vision
To ensure every young athlete has the chance to grow, compete, and lead—regardless of their family’s financial situation. By removing barriers that hold kids back, we help them fully experience the power of sports and strengthen the pipeline of future leaders across the country.

### Mission
To open doors for youth programs through a fundraising experience families and organizations can trust. We combine reliable processes, innovative technology, and a commitment to real value so that every dollar raised directly supports better opportunities for young athletes.

### Why it matters
When clinics and tournaments have the resources to offer high-quality coaching, modern equipment, proper nutrition, and the ability to travel and compete at the highest levels, young athletes thrive—and communities benefit. Youth participation in sports has been consistently linked to stronger civic engagement such as volunteering and voting, and a greater likelihood of contributing positively to society.

## What’s in This Repo

This project powers:
- A modern, responsive marketing site for Sports Our Youth
- Donation experience (front-end UI)
- Store experience (front-end UI) allowing supporters to contribute by purchasing merch
- Impact storytelling (stats, testimonials, and program summaries)
- Contact + FAQ pages

> Note: Payment processing and fulfillment integrations may be represented as UI-only in early stages and enabled later.
